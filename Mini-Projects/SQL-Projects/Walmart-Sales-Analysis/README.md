# Walmart Sales Analysis (SQL Project)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Walmart sales data using SQL to extract meaningful business insights.
The objective is to understand customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and branch-level sales trends.

This analysis helps businesses make data-driven decisions related to sales strategy, product demand, and operational performance.

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains transactional sales data from Walmart stores.

Key columns included in the dataset:

* Invoice ID – Unique transaction identifier
* Branch – Store branch where the sale occurred
* City – City of the branch
* Customer Type – Member or Normal customer
* Gender – Customer gender
* Product Line – Category of product purchased
* Unit Price – Price per unit of product
* Quantity – Number of units sold
* Tax – Tax applied to the transaction
* Total – Total bill amount
* Date – Purchase date
* Time – Purchase time
* Payment – Payment method used
* COGS – Cost of goods sold
* Gross Income – Profit generated
* Rating – Customer satisfaction rating

---

## Business Problems Solved

The following business questions were analyzed using SQL:

* Which payment method is most frequently used by customers?
* What is the highest rated product category in each branch?
* Which day of the week has the highest number of sales transactions?
* What time of day generates the most sales?
* Which branch performs best in terms of sales?

---

## SQL Concepts Used

The analysis uses several important SQL concepts:

* SELECT statements
* GROUP BY aggregation
* ORDER BY sorting
* Aggregate functions (SUM, AVG, COUNT)
* CASE statements
* Date and time functions
* Filtering using WHERE clause
